# Vision

## Why CellarMind?

Most cellar management software focuses on inventory.

CellarMind has a different goal:

> Help wine lovers decide **what to drink, when to drink it, and why**.

Inventory management is only one part of the project.

CellarMind combines:

- cellar management
- wine knowledge
- maturity estimation
- provenance of information
- personal tasting history
- decision support

## Principles

CellarMind should always be:

- transparent
- reproducible
- explainable
- offline-first
- privacy-first

The user's cellar always belongs to the user.

## Long-term vision

CellarMind should become a personal assistant capable of answering questions such as:

- What should I drink this year?
- Which bottles are close to their peak?
- Which wines should I keep another decade?
- What is the estimated value of my cellar?
- Which bottle best matches tonight's dinner?
- How can I organize my cellar?

without requiring any external SaaS.

## Non-goals

CellarMind is **not**

- a social network
- an online cellar
- a marketplace
- a recommendation engine based on other users

It is your own cellar intelligence.
